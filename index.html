The importance of Deep Breathing

Breathing helps reduce stress

When you become anxious or stressed your brain releases cortisol which is a hormone related to stress. When you take deep breaths it slows your heart rate down and more oxygen enters the blood stream which helps the brain relax. Breathing also increases endorphins which also lower stress and anxiety.

Breathing to improve your health

Breathing improves blood flow and also cleanses the body of toxins. When your body has the proper amount of oxygen it carries and absorbs nutrients and vitamins more efficiently. This improves immunity from illness.

Deep Breathing:

  • Reduces toxins
  • Increases immunity
  • Reduces stress

Breathing and Meditation as a tool to help focus

Focused breathing is a good way to improve your focus and concentration. In a recent study it was found that deep breathing combined with meditation helps stave off depression and dementia. Research done on children who practiced meditation as part of their currriculm have experienced wide ranging mental and physical health benefits.

Breathing combined with Meditation:

  1. Improves concentration
  2. Helps with mental health
  3. Has physical and mental health benefits for people of all ages
